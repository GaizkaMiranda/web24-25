Working since 1957

2010. After eleven years developing engineering projects under the Lorpen brand, Sistemas Arquimart was born, a company with a vocation for innovation and design that aims to revolutionize the concept and manufacturing of systems. compartmentalization and distribution for offices.

That same year, Sistemas Arquimart began marketing one of its great products: the Vitrum 35 screen, which immediately became a star product and key to the development of the company. Today, Vitrum 35 It is present in some of the facilities of large companies such as BBVA, Bankia, Mapfre, Nestlé, etc.

2012. Bilbao City Council commissions MB Arquitectos y Sistemas Arquimart with the interior distribution of its new offices in the city. The result: 2,000 meters of installation by the Vitrum 35 model. It was consolidated thus the first major project of the company.

A year later, Impuls 100 was born, a modular partition system with excellent acoustic properties and very versatile that has been very well received in the market. Its simplicity and characteristics (high performance, elegance and functionality) They would earn him the immediate recognition of those who opt for minimalism without giving up privacy.

2014. Aware of the potential of wood as an additional element to metal and glass in spaces dedicated to work and recreation, Sistemas Arquimart embarks on the purchase of a carpentry company and begins to design and manufacture special furniture in this type of material.

The incorporation of wood in the design and manufacture of compartmentalization elements allows Sistemas Arquimart to tackle more ambitious and innovative projects. Customized conception and production is now added to the screens and cabinets of stands, visual dividers, ticket offices, offices, exhibitors, etc., designed for meetings and dialogue.
